2006

Founded in 2006 in Kaohsiung, Taiwan; Gong Cha, which translates to “tribute tea for the emperor” opened its doors into a highly saturated bubble tea market.

2009

Since 2009, has swept Malaysia’s neighboring countries – Singapore, Hong Kong, China, Macau and the Philippines – by storm.
To date, Gong Cha is the fastest growing brand and has further expanded internationally to South Korea, Japan, USA, Canada and other countries, with more than 1,500 stores worldwide. Gong Cha’s story is one that roots itself as one of the most reliable providers of quality tea in the world.

2011

Gong Cha was first established in Malaysia by Teafe (M) Sdn Bhd since April, 2011 and instantly became phenomenally popular. In a market that has become increasingly saturated with bubble tea chains, many brands have continued to close its stores. However, Gong Cha continues to operate steadily with strict control over the quality of its products.

2012

As of 2012, it had about forty-six stores in Hong Kong, mainly in MTR stations and shopping centers

2017

In January 2017, Gong Cha's main headquarters was acquired by Gong Cha Korea. CEO Kim Soo-Min has announced plans to reach out into the Middle East and Latin America, while promising to "change everything but the taste".
