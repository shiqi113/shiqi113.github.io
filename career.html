Career
Location Bangsar South
Position Operations Executive
Detail
  • Ensure compliance of Company SOP, policies and procedures by outlets.
  • To implement, organise and conduct audits to ensure highest level of service at outlets.
  • Assist Operation Manager in optimising outlet sales.
  • Responsible for new product development and implementation.
  • Assist marketing team for any campaign planning and implementation.
  • To ensure effective management, motivation, training and development of all team members employed in the service outlet.
  • Attend to customer inquiries and complaint; work with management and outlet to improve customer satisfaction.
  • Assist in new outlet opening to ensure smooth operations after handover.
  • To ensure facilities comply with all relevant legislation relating to health & safety, food hygiene regulations & licensing legislation.
Requirements:
  • Candidate must possess at least Diploma or Bachelor's Degree in Food & Beverage Services Management, Hospitality/Tourism/Hotel Management or equivalent.
  • Required language(s): English, Chinese
  • Able to work under pressure and multi-task in a fast paced environment.
  • Good interpersonal skill and communication skill.
  • Candidate must be prepared to travel to all stores within Malaysia. Possessing own transport will be added advantage.

Detail
  • To assist the store in terms of cashier, bar and kitchen.
