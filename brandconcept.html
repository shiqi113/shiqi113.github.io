Our Mission & Vision

To inspire the human spirit and create happiness.

Our Promise


Cooperating with professional factories,
we harvest only tea leaves of the best quality
with a taste unique to Gong Cha.
Every batch of tea distributed is certified by SGS International.

Our commitment to freshness is uncompromising,we brew our teas every four hours for optimal flavour and freshness.

We adopt a high standard of water purification facilities, where the water we use to brew tea, produce ice cubes and prepare drinks are totally bactericidal. Consumers may rest assured that all of our products are hygienic and safe.

We value your wants and needs, so we tailor every cup to your individual taste and sweetness preference.


Our Value


Quality

We select only premium quality tea leaves and ingredients to create satisfaction beyond expec­tations. Gong cha manages the entire process of tea production from the selection of tea leaves to cultivation, drying, fermentation, and purification.


Respect

In our stores, we greet everyone warmly and sincerely to create happiness not only in every cup, but in every heart. We seek to treat our customers and team members with integrity and dignity.


Integrity

We provide dependable and high quality products with a commitment to maintaining transparency.


Innovation

We aspire to inspire and make the ordinary extraordinary. We continue to stand at the forefront of trends and to challenge the norms with innovation and creativity.


Empathy

We have an inclusive vision and strive to create a culture of warmth to positively influence the people and communities around us.
